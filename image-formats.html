Image Formats

Raster Images

Raster images are made up of lots of little different colored pixels that come together to form a complete image. Almost all of the photos found on the web and in print are raster images, and JPEGs, GIFs, and PNGs are common raster image types

Vector Images

Vector images, on the other hand, are constructed using paths that each have a mathematical formula, rather than individual pixels. This makes them infinitely scalable. Vector images are perfect for things like logos, where you would want to use the same image at many different sizes, from business cards to billboard
